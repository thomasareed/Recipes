# Popovers

Makes 6 popovers

## Ingredients

* 1 cup all-purpose flour
* 1/4 tsp. salt
* 1 tsp. sugar
* 1 tbsp. butter, melted and cooled
* 1 cup milk (room temperature)
* 2 eggs (room temperature)

## Instructions

Preheat oven to 425Â°. Grease popover pans. In a bowl, stir together flour, salt and sugar until thoroughly blended. Add butter, milk and eggs. Beat with a whisk until very smooth. Fill popover pans to about half-full. Bake for 30 minutes. DO NOT OPEN THE OVEN! If you do, they will fall.
