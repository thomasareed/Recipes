# Hummus

## Ingredients

* 1 can garbanzo beans (drain and reserve liquid)
* 2-4 cloves garlic
* juice of 1/2 lemon
* 2 tbsp tahini paste
* 1 tsp salt
* 1/4 tsp cumin
* black pepper to taste

## Instructions

Add all ingredients except garbanzo liquid to food processor bowl. Process until smooth, adding garbanzo liquid as necessary.
