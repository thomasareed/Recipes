# Corn casserole

Quick and easy, kind of like a soft corn bread with lots of corn in it.

## Ingredients

* 1/2 cup butter
* 2 eggs
* 8.5 oz corn bread mix
* 15 oz canned corn
* 15 oz cream corn
* 1 cup sour cream

## Instructions

Mix ingredients. Pour into 9 inch square baking dish. Bake at 350 Â° for 45 minutes.
